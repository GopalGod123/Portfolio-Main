About Me

Hello, I'm Gopal Khandelwal

Gopal Khandelwal 3D Generalist Artist As a skilled 3D Generalist Artist with a robust background in animation, character creation, and UI/UX design, I bring a diverse set of talents to the creative field. My expertise lies in crafting high-quality 3D characters from scratch, with a focus on texturing, rigging, and animation. I excel in creating intricate Sci-Fi looping animations and engaging Crypto NFT arts, as well as optimizing 3D assets through weight painting and polygon reduction. With hands-on experience across multiple projects and roles, I have contributed significantly to renowned projects such as "Planet Eaters," where I led character design, animation, and visual effects. My background also includes impactful freelance work on Fiverr and YURU, where I honed my skills in motion graphics and 3D modeling. In my current role at Shivai-Infotech, I integrate my expertise as both a full-stack developer and 3D Generalist, creating and rigging 3D models, producing promotional videos, and managing multifaceted projects in a dynamic environment. My experience with software like Blender, Cinema4D, Clo3D, and Unreal Engine, coupled with my background in UI/UX design, enables me to deliver visually compelling and technically sound work. I hold a B-Tech in Computer Science Engineering from the University of Petroleum and Energy Studies, which laid the foundation for my technical and creative skills. With a track record of successful project contributions and a dedication to continuous learning, I am passionate about pushing the boundaries of 3D art and design.
